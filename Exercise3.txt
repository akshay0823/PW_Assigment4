3.Define accessibility in the context Of web development. Discuss why it's essential to create accessible
websites and how it benefits different user groups
Accessibilty in Web Development: Accessibilty in web development refers to designing and coding websites
in a way that ensures people with disabilities can perceive, understand, navigate, and interact with digital
content effectively. It Involves making web content inclusive and usable for everyone, rogardless of their
abilities.

Importance of Accessible Websites:

1.Inclusivity: Accessible websites ensure that everyone, including people with disabilities, can access and use
online information and services.
Legal Compliance: Many countries have laws requiring websites to be accessible, avoiding legal issues and
promoting equal access.

2.User Experience: Accessible design often leads to improved user experiences for everyone, including clear
navigation and content structure.
Business Growth: Accessible websites reach a wider audienco, increasing engagement loyalty, and potential
customer base.

Benefits for User Groups:

1.Visual impairments: Accessibility provldes screen reader support and text alternatives, aiding blind and
visually impaired users.

2.Hearing impairments: Captions and transcrlpts benefit those with hearing impairments by making audio
content understandable.

3.Motor Disabilities: Keyboard navigation and easy-to-cllck elements assist users with motor limitations.
Cognitive Disablitles: Accossiblo design simplifies content and layout, aiding individuals with cognitive
challenges,

4.Eldery Users: Accessible websites accommodate age-related limitations, ensuring usability for elderly users.

5.Mobile Users: Accessible design improves moblle experiences through simplified and responsive layouts.
